We Suck

It's no suprise that the Detroit Tigers are thought to be a bottom 10 team heading into this year.

To their credit, even the Detroit Tigers hope that their future is much brighter than what we currently have.

The Detroit Tigers were last in their division in 2020.

That should make this season somewhat more bearable if we as fans can focus on the future rather than the hardships we're enduring to get there.
