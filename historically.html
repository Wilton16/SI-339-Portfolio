A Good Decade

The 2010s has seen the Tigers perform somewhat well, as we are the second best team in our division in the Decades Standings with the majority of our losses coming from more recent years.

We also had multiple playoff appearences and a World Series appearence.

Furthermore, the Detroit Tigers have had a number of exciting players that led us to our highest points over the last decade, whom we celebrated and enjoyed watching.

Most of these All Stars have departed, leaving Detroit desperate for new athletic icons.
